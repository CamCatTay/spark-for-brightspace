Spark for Brightspace

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Spark for Brightspace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension interacts with student data and your personal information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our extension accesses student data solely to provide its intended functionality. However, we do not store, transmit, or share any student data in any form. All data processing occurs locally within your browser.

We do collect anonymized usage data to improve functionality and troubleshoot issues. The collected data includes:

  • Number of successful extension loads
  • Number of custom tasks created
  • Number of Gradescope syncs
  • Current streak number
  • Institution's public Canvas/Blackboard/Brightspace URL

3. Anonymized Data Collection and Transmission

While we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or student data, we do collect and transmit anonymized usage data as outlined above. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information and cannot be linked to individual users.

4. Third-Party Services

Our extension does not use or integrate with third-party services that collect, process, or store student data.

5. Security

Since no student data is stored or transmitted, there is no risk of unauthorized access to student data through our extension. However, users should ensure their devices and browsers are secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our extension is designed to comply with student data privacy regulations. As we do not collect, store, or share student data, there is no risk to children's privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our GitHub repository.
